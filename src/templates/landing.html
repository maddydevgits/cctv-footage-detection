Decentralized CCTV Footage Monitoring and Management System using IPFS


The project addresses the vulnerability of CCTV footage as evidence of tampering by proposing a robust solution leveraging blockchain technology. In response to fraudulent activities attempting to manipulate video evidence, the system employs a decentralized file storage system IPFS, and utilizes a hashing technique. The proposed system ensures the privacy and integrity of video clips, marking a significant advancement in securing reliable video evidence for legal proceedings.

Building upon existing solutions, the project introduces innovative features, including selective encryption for privacy protection, tamper-proof blockchain storage, and decentralized sharing with access control. The diverse range of technologies involved, such as blockchain, and IPFS, collectively contribute to a trustworthy and efficient system for storing and verifying CCTV footage.
